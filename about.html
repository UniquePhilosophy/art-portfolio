About Me

I am a Multidimensional Artist with an Educational background in Human Rights Law, dismayed by the system.
I’m a mentally suffocated Neurodivergent butterfly turned, Existential mind obsessed wanderer.
Thus, I am consumed by contemplations of freedom, justice, solidarity, humanity, stars, being, and the will within consciousness.
I am:
An Unraveling Chrysalis.
A Rising Phoenix.
Poetically philosophical.
A melancholic dream.
Ultimately, I’m just a girl.
A human learning being.
And hopefully, or already, a butterfly effect in human form.
And that’s what my art is and will be all about.
